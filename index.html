Description: A diminutive magical goblet casts a protective shield from the central city, CityNameGoesHere. Human and demon forces can only attack cities that are within the neutral zone between the goblet shield and the demonic influence barrier. When either side captures a city, it gains influence within the neutral zone. The demonic influence is weaker for cities closer to the central city. The neutral zone barriers are set a fixed distance away from the closest captured city of the opposite side.

This is a fan pre-production prototype of something vaguely related to the game MASSIVE CHALICE. I am not affiliated with MASSIVE CHALICE except as a backer.
